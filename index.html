    📖 How to Play

    The coop is under attack!
    You are the Battle Chicken — the farm's last defender. Forest creatures — foxes 🦊, wolves 🐺, feral dogs 🐶, and sneaky cats 😼 — are trying to take over the coop!

    Your weapon? Eggs. Yes, regular eggs. But don't worry — they're surprisingly effective. 🥚💥

    🎮 Controls

    ⬅️➡️Arrow keys or A / D — move
    🥚Spacebar or 🔫 button — shoot
    ⏸️P — pause the game

    ✨ Power-ups

    🌽Corn — speed boost + egg damage up
    🌾Wheat — restores 1 health point

    ⚠️ Watch out!

    • ❤️ Enemies deal damage on contact
    • 🚨 Let 10 enemies pass — game over!
    • 📈 Enemies get faster and tougher as your score grows — don't forget to grab 🌽 Corn to boost your speed and egg damage, or you won't be able to defeat them!
    • 👹 Score enough points and face the BOSS...

    Good luck, soldier! Defend the coop! 🐔🛡️

    📄 Privacy Policy

    📄 Privacy Policy

    What we collect

    When you save your score, we store:

    • Nickname — the name you enter
    • Score & play time — your game results
    • Device ID — a random identifier stored on your device (or your Telegram ID if playing via Telegram)
    • Timestamp — when the score was saved

    Why we collect it

    Solely to display the leaderboard. Nothing else.

    Third parties

    Your data is stored on Supabase (EU region, Frankfurt/Paris). We do not sell or share your data.

    Your rights (GDPR)

    You have the right to:

    • 🔍 Access — see what data we have about you
    • 🗑️ Delete — request deletion of your data
    • ❌ Refuse — play without saving scores

    How to delete your data

    Press the "🗑️ Delete My Data" button in the main menu, or contact us at: contact@iliyaglazunov.com

    Cookies & Local Storage

    We use localStorage to store your device ID and local high score. No tracking cookies are used.
