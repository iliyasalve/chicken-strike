📖 How to Play

The coop is under attack!
You are the Battle Chicken — the farm's last defender. Forest creatures — foxes 🦊, wolves 🐺, feral dogs 🐶, and sneaky cats 😼 — are trying to take over the coop!

Your weapon? Eggs. Yes, regular eggs. But don't worry — they're surprisingly effective. 🥚💥

🎮 Controls

⬅️➡️Arrow keys or A / D — move
🥚Spacebar or 🔫 button — shoot
⏸️P — pause the game

✨ Power-ups

🌽Corn — speed boost + egg damage up
🌾Wheat — restores 1 health point

⚠️ Watch out!

  • ❤️ Enemies deal damage on contact
  • 🚨 Let 10 enemies pass — game over!
  • 📈 Enemies get faster and tougher as your score grows — don't forget to grab 🌽 Corn to boost your speed and egg damage, or you won't be able to defeat them!
  • 👹 Score enough points and face the BOSS...

Good luck, soldier! Defend the coop! 🐔🛡️
